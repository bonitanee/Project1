Each year, textile waste takes up to four percent of Canadian landfills, that’s equivalent to three B.C. Place Stadiums or a Toronto Sky Dome full of clothing. At Closet Relay, we are passionate about sustainability and we hope to help reduce textile waste and encourage community engagement by giving a new life to once loved clothing.
